Giving Back

Community Service

Service is at the heart of Beta Alpha Psi. Our chapter puts professional skills to work for the community through ongoing outreach programs.

VITA Tax Preparation

Each spring, our members volunteer as IRS-certified tax preparers through the Volunteer Income Tax Assistance program. We provide free tax preparation services to low-income individuals and families in the Birmingham area who might not otherwise have access to professional help.

Members earn IRS certification and gain real-world tax experience.

Financial Literacy Outreach

We partner with local high schools to teach students the fundamentals of personal finance — budgeting, saving, understanding credit, and planning for college expenses. Our members develop and lead interactive workshops throughout the school year.

Reaching the next generation with practical financial skills.

Service is a core requirement of BAP membership. Beta Alpha Psi's national organization emphasizes community service hours as a key component of chapter activity. Our members consistently exceed the national minimum, reflecting our chapter's commitment to using professional knowledge for public good.

Eligibility

Beta Alpha Psi is open to students who meet the following criteria:

  • Enrolled in accounting, finance, or information systems at Samford University
  • Completed at least one upper-level course in your major
  • Maintain a minimum 3.0 overall GPA and 3.25 major GPA
  • Committed to attending chapter meetings and completing service hours

How to Join

We accept new members each fall and spring semester. Attend our kickoff meeting to learn more, or reach out to our officers to get started. Active membership requires participation in professional events, technical workshops, and community service activities throughout the semester.
